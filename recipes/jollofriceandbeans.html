Jollof Rice and Beans

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 cup beans
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice and beans separately under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they become fragrant and translucent.
  4. Add the diced bell pepper and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the pepper softens.
  5. Add the rinsed beans to the pot and stir to combine with the sautéed vegetables.
  6. Pour in the chicken broth, and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the beans are tender.
  7. Once the beans are cooked, add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir to combine with the beans and vegetables.
  8. Cover the pot again and let it simmer for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and has absorbed the liquid.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and give it a final stir before serving.
