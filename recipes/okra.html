Okra

Ingredients

  • 1 lb okra, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the bell pepper and okra, and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes, or until the okra is tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
